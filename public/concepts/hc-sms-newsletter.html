08 — 6-MONTH ROADMAP

Crawl. Walk.
Run.

We don't start by talking to consumers directly. We start by leveraging the relationships dispensaries already have — then gradually earn the right to own the conversation ourselves.
Phase 01 — Months 1–2
Crawl
Borrow the audience.
Build the foundation.
01
Alpine IQ Dispensary Campaigns
Partner with dispensaries through Alpine IQ to reach their existing customer base. These are warm, high-intent consumers already in the store ecosystem.
↓
02
Drive to CURADOR Site
Campaign traffic lands on a CURADOR-branded page — not the dispensary's. We own the destination from day one.
↓
03
Capture Email + Build Awareness
Light opt-in offer. Collect email as the primary asset. Begin sending brand awareness content — not product pushes. The goal is list growth and trust.
Phase 02 — Months 3–4
Walk
Start talking directly.
Grow in-house.
01
Ramp Into Direct-to-Consumer
As the list grows, we reduce reliance on Alpine IQ campaigns and begin communicating directly with our own audience. The brand voice takes over.
↓
02
In-House List Growth
The team activates its own acquisition channels — in-house rewards programs and on-site email capture to grow the list independently of dispensary partnerships.
↓
03
Launch Newsletter + Light SMS
CURADOR Signal launches. Drop alerts begin via SMS. Audience segmentation kicks in — consumers, budtenders, and early VIPs start receiving tailored content.
Phase 03 — Months 5–6
Run
Own the channel.
Scale the system.
01
Full Automation + VIP Flows
Behavior-based automation kicks in. Welcome flows, re-engagement sequences, and VIP early access become self-running systems.
↓
02
Geo-Targeted Messaging
Location-aware SMS and email campaigns tie availability directly to nearby dispensaries. The right message reaches the right person in the right market.
↓
03
Scale Across All CURADOR Brands
The system built on Head Change becomes the template. Every brand in the CURADOR portfolio inherits the infrastructure, the playbook, and the audience.
